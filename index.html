About Us

Recitation Refinement is a Qur'anic Education Group that serves the need of Adult Muslims who wish to take the path of learning how to recite the Qur'an the way in which it was revealed, and to also embark upon the journey of memorisation. The objective of this programme is for us all to become from the People of the Qur'an. Hence, there is also much emphasis is placed upon the nurturing and self-development of students through different initiatives. Each student conducts 1-2-1 Classes for their Recitation/Memorisation, however all other initiatives and programmes are group-based.

Why Recitation Refinement?

A Purposeful Journey

We are a group of students that are on a journey of learning the recitation of the Qur'an with the objective of being from Allah's People!

Tailored to your Needs

We deliver 1-2-1, bespoke classes that focus on your Recitation & Memorisation of the Qur’an with a clear pathway of your learning.

Self-Development

Weekly Group Seminars are delivered to provide character development; an essential aspect of studying the Qur’an!

Spiritual Boosters

We organise monthly gatherings that aim to provide spiritual reminders with external guest speakers!
